Payroll Schedule App

Unofficial app to view the payroll schedule on your watch.

The Payroll Schedule Calendars published by the U.S. Department of the Interior are available here.

The Payroll Schedule app is an unofficial app that allows viewing information about the current pay period directly on your Apple watch.

To add the Payroll Schedule complication to a watch face perform the following steps:

  1. Long press on the watch face until the Edit button shows up:
  2. Swipe left to get to the rightmost screen which allows selecting the complications to display on the watchface:
  3. Tap on the location where you want the pay period to display and scroll to select the Payroll Schedule complication.
  4. Press the crown of your watch to go back to the watchface:
  5. Now you can instantly view the current pay period and tap it to view additional information.
